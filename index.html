Bio

As a passionate Software Engineer, I am driven to create innovative and high-quality software solutions. I draw inspiration from books such as “Clean Code” and “The Clean Coder” by Robert C. Martin, “Effective Java: Second Edition” by Joshua Bloch, and “Extreme Programming Explained” by Kent Beck. My goal is to be recognized as a respected professional in the software development industry.

I have extensive experience developing modern enterprise applications using the Spring Framework and JavaScript frameworks like Node.js and Express.js, and I have a strong appreciation for XP and TDD methodologies. My diverse background, including experience in both public and private industries, such as the Spanish and UK governments and the e-commerce sector, has given me a broad range of knowledge and expertise in working on large-scale projects and delivering high-demand services and platforms.

I have also been fortunate to work with the UK Government’s Ministry of Justice, where I played a crucial role in digitizing paper-based processes. I am always eager to learn and take on new challenges, and I am confident in my ability to deliver outstanding results.
